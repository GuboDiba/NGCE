Who we are

Nomad Girl Child Empowerment (NGCE) is a community-based organization formed in 2021 with the aim of eradicating child marriages, teenage pregnancies, and female genital mutilation (FGM) to restore the dignity of young girls in society. The organization is based in Marsabit County in Northern Kenya and is a non-membership organization. The nomads’ communities have limited awareness and respect of rights related to women’s empowerment. Through NGCE, women are empowered with new life skills to express themselves and set and pursue their goals without being held back by cultural and stereotyped limitations. To that effect, NGCE seeks to address these traditional ideas of what women’s or girls’ roles should be in nomadic communities that hold back girls and women from contributing to important development goals through educational empowerment beyond the secondary level, rather than getting married before they achieve their fruitful dreams.

Corporate information

Vision


To see a community where girls overcome gender-based barriers to fully and meaningfully participate in development initiatives

Mission


To provide a supportive environment that educate and inspire underserved girls in pastrolist community to achieve their full potential

Objectives

  1. To reduce the rate of child marriages and teenage pregnancies and restore the dignity of young girls in the society
  2. To mobilize and sensitize community members to initiate open dialogues amongst the elders,women,girls,men and boys to accelerate the abandonment of child marriages and female genital mutilation.
  3. To promote gender equality in all aspects of women and girl's lives which includes eliminating child and forced marriages and securing equal participation and opportunity through advocacy and awareness.
  4. To support and promote girls' access to education through the provision of sanitary towel,exercise books,undergarments and other basic need to orphans and vulnerable girls.
  5. To support the transformation of girls through capacity building,training in gender,HIV/AIDS.human rights,leadership,and confidence-building.
